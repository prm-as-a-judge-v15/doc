Dense Robotic Auditing

Turn rollout videos into interpretable progress diagnostics with one command.

PRM-as-a-Judge v1.5 starts from a JSONL manifest and works out of the box with release-ready defaults. Advanced users can tune Dopamine modes, multi-view inputs, goal images, multi-GPU execution, smoothing, and custom PRM backends.
